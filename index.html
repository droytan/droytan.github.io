Chocolate Truffles

  • Time:30 minutes
  • Serves: 1 cup

Utensils

  • Refrigerator
  • Blender
  • Stove
Ingredient and Quantity
  • 1 package of oreo cookie
  • 1 package of cream cheese(190 g)
  • Cocolate to melt

    • Step 1: Crush the cookies until they are powdery.
    Step 2:Mix the cookie powder with the cream cheese utnil you get a dough.
    Step 3:Form balls,dip them in the melted chocolate and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

    Chocolate cake

    • Time:30-35 minutes

    Utensils

    • Oven
    • Blender
    • Bowl
    Ingredient and Quantity
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Step 1:Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl, sift together the flour,cocoa sugar and baking powder.
    Step 2:Add the wet ingredients, the milk, eggs,oil and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients.Stir until everything is well combined and you have a smooth batter.
    Step 3:Bake, pour the batter into a grased cake pan.Bake at 180°C(350°F) for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
